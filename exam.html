Frequently Asked Questions
1. Can i appear in TS EAMCET if i belong to Bihar?

Top Answer By Priyanka Joshi on 02 Jan 23

No other state student cannot apply for TS EAMCET as it is a state-level entrance exam conducted for candidates belonging to Telangana/ Andhra Pradesh. The conducting exam body of the entrance exam is JNTU Hyderabad. The eligibility criteria have clearly stated that the candidates applying for TS EAMCET must have a domicile of Telangana/Andhra Pradesh. If you are fulfilling the specified criteria, you can sit for the exam, otherwise, there are no chances. TS EAMCET witness approximately 2.16 Lakh applications per year. Read more
